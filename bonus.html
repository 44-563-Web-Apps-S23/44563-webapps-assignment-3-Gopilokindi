Wanda Maximoff is a fictional character primarily portrayed by Elizabeth Olsen
in the Marvel Cinematic Universe media franchise based on the Marvel Comics character
of the same name. Wanda is initially depicted as a Sokovian refugee who, along with her
twin brother Pietro, volunteers to be experimented on by Hydra.
